RACISM

-our advocacy-

Racism is a deep-rooted injustice that harms individuals, communities, and societies as a whole. It creates division based on skin color, ethnicity, and cultural backgrounds, leading to inequality, fear, and hatred. But we can CHANGE that. By embracing differences and diversity and recognizing our shared humanity, we can create a world where everyone is treated without discrimination and a world with justice, respect, and equality.
